About Us

Building a sustainable agricultural future for Nigeria and Africa

Our Story & Mission

Founded in Imo State, Nigeria, JOSHUA-RICH AGRO-TECH INDUSTRIES LIMITED emerged from a vision to revolutionize African agriculture through technology, innovation, and sustainable practices.

We bridge the gap between traditional farming methods and modern agri-tech solutions, empowering smallholder farmers and large-scale enterprises alike to achieve higher productivity, better yields, and sustainable growth.

Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and effective agricultural technology that addresses the unique challenges faced by African farmers while promoting environmental sustainability and economic prosperity.

Our Vision

To be the leading agro-tech company in Africa, driving agricultural transformation through innovation and sustainable practices.

Our Mission

To empower African farmers with cutting-edge technology, quality inputs, and expert guidance to achieve food security and economic prosperity.

Smallholder Farmers

Our solutions for smallholder farmers include affordable access to quality inputs, mobile-based advisory services, weather forecasting, market linkage platforms, and financial inclusion services. We understand the unique challenges faced by small-scale farmers and provide practical, cost-effective solutions that fit their needs and budget.

Key features include SMS-based crop advisories, group buying discounts, mobile money integration, and training programs on modern farming techniques.
